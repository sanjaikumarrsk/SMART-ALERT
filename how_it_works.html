How SMART ALERT Works

1. Real-Time Emergency Detection

The system uses AI models to detect emergencies directly from live camera feeds. It identifies situations such as accidents, fire outbreaks, explosions, and robbery attempts instantly without human input.

2. Automatic Alert Classification

After detecting an incident, the system automatically identifies the type of emergency. For example:
• Fire → Fire Department
• Accident → Ambulance & Police
• Robbery → Police
• Explosion → Fire, Police & Medical Teams

3. Instant Notification to Authorities

Once the emergency type is confirmed, SMART ALERT sends an instant alert notification directly to the required authorities with:
• Location details
• Camera ID
• Snapshot of the incident
• Severity level

4. Public Camera Monitoring (Government Users)

Government officials can monitor public area cameras such as traffic CCTVs, street cameras, and city surveillance networks. They can:
• View real-time alerts
• Monitor multiple locations
• Dispatch emergency teams instantly
• Access historical alerts for investigation

5. Home Surveillance Control (Citizen Users)

Home users can link their private CCTV cameras. In case of fire, break-ins, or suspicious activity, alerts are sent directly to the homeowner. They can:
• View their live camera feed
• Get instant alerts on their phone
• Contact police or fire services from the app

6. Advantages of SMART ALERT

• Extremely fast emergency detection and response
• Reduces loss of life and property
• Works with both public and private cameras
• Eliminates manual monitoring
• AI ensures high accuracy during detection
• Helps police and emergency teams act faster
